LexiPower helps words stick. It pairs short, focused practice with a review
rhythm that brings words back just as you are about to forget them — so study
time turns into lasting vocabulary.

## Practice that fits a few minutes

Run quick quizzes to check what you know, sharpen recall with spelling
challenges, and keep sessions short enough to do between other things. Small,
regular practice beats the occasional long cram.

## Review built for retention

LexiPower brings words back in review sessions spaced over time. Instead of
seeing everything at once, you revisit words right when a reminder helps most —
the simple idea behind durable, long-term memory.

## Build your own word list

Add the words you actually want to learn and grow a personal collection you can
practise and master at your own pace.
