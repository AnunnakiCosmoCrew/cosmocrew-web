SliceFocus turns your day into something you can see. Instead of a flat list of
to-dos, your hours sit on a radial 24-hour clock — so a single glance shows what
you have planned, where the gaps are, and how much of the day is left.

## Plan in slices

Block out time the way it actually feels: in slices around the clock. Drag to
shape a slice, label it, and the whole day rearranges itself visually. It is a
calmer way to plan than scrolling a vertical agenda.

## Focus, then rest

When it is time to work, start a focus session. SliceFocus runs Pomodoro-style
timers so you can settle into one slice at a time, take honest breaks, and keep
a steady rhythm through the day.

## See the whole day at once

The radial view keeps the big picture in front of you. Morning, afternoon, and
evening read as one continuous shape — easy to scan, easy to adjust, and quietly
motivating as the day fills in.
