Welcome to CosmoCrew.

We are a small, independent software studio. We like quiet, well-made tools that
do one thing clearly and respect your attention — and we build them one at a
time, with care.

Two apps are live today:

- **SliceFocus** — visual day planning and focus sessions on a 24-hour radial
  timeline.
- **LexiPower** — vocabulary practice through quizzes, spelling, and review
  sessions.

This page is where we will share occasional notes: meaningful updates to the
apps, small improvements, and the thinking behind them. No noise, no hype — just
the work as it ships.

Thanks for stopping by.
